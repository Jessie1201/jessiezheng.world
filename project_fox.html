Crimson Fox

As the course project from Physical Interaction Design and Realization, we made this sweet fox pet alive breathing! Arduino and 3d modeling are the primary techniques used.

This desk pet keeps you company while working, and panics by breathing heavily to remind you of taking a rest if you have been working overtime. So at this point, you should pet and comfort her, then she would breath normally again.

The body was made of leather, and aluminum foil was used to sense the capacity change. For technical details see our INSTRUCTABLES ❤.
