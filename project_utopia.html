World Beyond

This is a futuristic urban planning project based on the Neuperlach, which is a small city of Bavarian capital Munich. Not much digital design is involved so the project is not elaborated in detail. However, there it showcases our the crazy but fancy ideas of the environment we designed for people to live in the future ‚ù§.

In brief, the design topic was initially "Utopia", literally meaning a kind of fantastic state of life where everything is in harmony. The key proposal of our idea is a layered world, where people mainly live beyond, while the ground is back to the forest for plants and animals. It sketches the scenery where people balance perfectly between the fast-developing technology and Nature.

Multiple innovations were applied, such as the energy supplying and water cleaning tower, the transportation of backpack jet, and the open space lights from bioelectricity. In conclusion, we illustrated a dream, but a realistic one that might to happen ‚ù§.
